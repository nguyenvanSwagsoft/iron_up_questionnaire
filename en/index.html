Iron Tracker

In Africa, approximately 2 out of 3 children under the age of 5 suffer from anaemia* and iron deficiency is responsible for 1 out of 2 of these cases**.

*WHO, The global prevalence of anemia in 2011, 2015.
**WHO, Focusing on anaemie, 2004

Iron deficiency anemia can be linked to a lack of iron in the child’s diet. This tool helps you identify and follow iron intake status of your children. Find Dietary tips to improve iron intake and support their iron needs.
